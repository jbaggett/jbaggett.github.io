## Datasets — OpenIntro

The bundled datasets in `data/` are derived from the **openintro** R package,
which accompanies *Introduction to Modern Statistics* (IMS) by Mine
Cetinkaya-Rundel and Johanna Hardin.

- **Source**: https://www.openintro.org/data/
- **R package**: https://github.com/OpenIntroStat/openintro
- **License**: GPL-3 (data), CC BY-SA 3.0 (textbook content)
- **Citation**: Cetinkaya-Rundel M, Hardin J, Diez D (2024). *openintro:
  Datasets and Supplemental Functions from OpenIntro Textbooks and Labs*.
  R package. https://www.openintro.org

The datasets have been reformatted to JSON for browser consumption. Variable
names, values, and structure are preserved from the original R package.
