Are You Psychic?

Predict 20 coin flips, then see how your results compare to what we'd expect from random guessing. Link your results to a simulation to test whether your score is statistically significant.

How to play
Click Heads or Tails before each flip. After 20 trials, you'll see your score and an interpretation.
What to look for
With random guessing, you'd expect about 10 out of 20 correct. The question is whether your result is far enough from 10 to rule out luck.
