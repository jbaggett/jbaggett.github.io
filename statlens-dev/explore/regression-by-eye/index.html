Help

Drag the green line to fit the data as closely as you can. Use the controls to reveal residuals and compare your line to the mathematically optimal fit.

Grab the line
Grab near an end to pivot; grab near the middle to shift parallel
Show Residuals
Show vertical lines from each point to your line
Show Squared Residuals
Show literal squares whose area equals the squared error at each point
Absolute / Squared
Switch between Sum of Absolute Errors (SAE) and Sum of Squared Errors (SSE)
Show Best-Fit Line
Reveal the mathematically optimal line for the current metric
