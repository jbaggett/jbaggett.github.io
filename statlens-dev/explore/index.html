Explore ToolsStatLens

Interactive tools for exploring and summarizing data.

  • One Quantitative Variable

    Summary statistics, histograms, dotplots, and boxplots for a single numeric variable.

  • One Categorical Variable

    Bar chart and frequency table for a single categorical variable.

  • Two Quantitative Variables

    Scatterplot with regression line, correlation, R-squared, and residual plot.

  • Two Categorical Variables

    Contingency table with proportion toggles and grouped bar charts.

  • Compare Groups

    Side-by-side boxplots, dotplots, or histograms comparing a quantitative variable across groups.

  • Dotplot Editor

    Click to add and remove points on a number line. Mean, median, SD, and IQR update live.

  • Data Explorer

    Load a dataset, select variables, and choose your own visualization. Practice the EDA workflow.

  • Are You Psychic?

    Predict coin flips to test your "psychic" powers, then use simulation to see if your result could be due to chance.

  • Stump the Chump

    Try to fake a random coin-flip sequence, then compare it to a real one. Can you fool the computer?
